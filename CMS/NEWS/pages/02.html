Welcomes Elin Kröger Nygren as New Chief Technology Officer

Aunetic, a leading provider of governance software solutions, is delighted to announce the appointment of Elin Kröger Nygren as its new Chief Technology Officer (CTO), effective March 4, 2024. Elin brings a wealth of experience and expertise in product development and enterprise architecture, which will be pivotal in driving Aunetic's growth strategy and enhancing its governance suite.

With a proven track record in technology leadership and innovation, Elin Kröger Nygren is the perfect fit to lead Aunetic's technological initiatives to new heights. Her deep understanding of enterprise architecture, combined with her strategic vision, will play a crucial role in shaping Aunetic's future development roadmap.

Prior to joining Aunetic, Elin held prominent roles in technology leadership, where she spearheaded transformative initiatives that aligned technology with business objectives. Her experience and insights will be invaluable at Aunetic.

Christian Baumgart, CEO of Aunetic: “We are thrilled to welcome Elin to the Aunetic team. Her extensive experience and her proven leadership skills make her the ideal candidate to lead our technology efforts. As we continue to expand our footprint in the governance software market, Elin's expertise will be instrumental in driving innovation and supporting our overall portfolio governance solutions strategy.”

I am excited to join Aunetic. Aunetic has established itself as a leader in governance software, and I am eager to leverage my experience to further enhance our suite of solutions and drive innovation. I look forward to working with the talented team at Aunetic to deliver value to our customers and support the company's growth objectives.

Elin Kröger Nygren
 CTO of Aunetic
