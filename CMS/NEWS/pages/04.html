Aunetic Acquires Swiss Legal and Regulatory Intelligence Provider LARI Digital

We are excited to announce the successful acquisition of LARI Digital, a prominent software provider offering a cloud-based legal and regulatory intelligence platform.

Founded in 2017 in Switzerland, LARI digital offers a leading cloud-based legal and regulatory intelligence platform. LARI is an end-to-end solution for in-house legal and compliance teams to easily monitor legislative and regulatory changes, while increasing the level of automation (not only by AI) and also translating them into actionable and auditable items. Regulatory monitoring tasks that otherwise would have been carried out manually are automated, and LARI’s users are able to gain more control over their workstreams.

Christian Baumgart, CEO of Aunetic: “I am delighted and proud to be taking the next step in the journey of building Aunetic’s development path so quickly. With the expertise of LARI, Aunetic is consistently pursuing one overarching goal: to become the Leading European Provider of Governance Technology. Personally, I find three advantages from the acquisition: excellent product fit with the existing products of Aunetic, increasing our presence in Switzerland and the high level of automation expertise together with fresh knowledge in the field of AI. I am excited about our future successes and assure you that this is not the last chapter in our growth story!”
